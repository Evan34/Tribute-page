John Ronald Reuel Tolkien was an English writer, poet, philologist, and university professor.

He is best known for his novels The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings

  • 1959 - Tolkien retired from university. In the years that followed, he acquired a growing celebrity as a writer.
  • 1973 - He died on September 2, at the age of 81.
